The Lavender Saloon is in business! In 1906 it will open to become the Black Cat Bar, one of the nations first LGBTQ+ bar. When the state of California tried suspending their liquer license in the 1950's so the owners took the case to the supreme court for discrimination and won their case, regaining their liquer license. This win paved the way to the opening of the Stonewall Inn which pushed forward the Queer liberation movement. You're a part of history!
